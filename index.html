My Favorite Musicians

Beyoncé

Beyoncé is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. She is known for her powerful vocals and dynamic performances. Beyoncé's music is a mix of R&B, pop, and hip hop, often with themes of love, relationships, and empowerment.

Most Streamed Song: "Halo"

BTS

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band that has gained international fame for their music and messages of self-love and social commentary. Their music spans various genres, including pop, hip-hop, and R&B.

Most Streamed Song: "Dynamite"

Bad Bunny

Bad Bunny is a Puerto Rican reggaeton and trap singer. His music is known for its catchy rhythms and melodies, often focusing on themes like love, partying, and social issues. Bad Bunny has played a significant role in popularizing Latin trap music globally.

Most Streamed Song: "Me Porto Bonito"
