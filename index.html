Archon's Favorite Artists

Learn about Chris Tomlin, Jeremy Camp, and Phil Wickham

Chris Tomlin

Chris Tomlin is one of the most well-known Christian artists in the world. He has written and performed many worship songs that are sung in churches everywhere. Some of his most popular songs include "How Great Is Our God," "Good Good Father," and "Our God."

Jeremy Camp

Jeremy Camp is a Christian singer and songwriter who has inspired millions with his music. His songs often reflect his personal journey of faith and hope. Popular songs by Jeremy Camp include "I Still Believe," "Walk by Faith," and "There Will Be a Day."

Phil Wickham

Phil Wickham is a worship leader and songwriter known for his powerful voice and heartfelt lyrics. His music encourages people to connect with God. Some of his well-loved songs are "Living Hope," "This Is Amazing Grace," and "Battle Belongs."
